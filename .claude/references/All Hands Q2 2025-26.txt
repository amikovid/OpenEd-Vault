Q2 Insights & Learnings
11 Biz Staff Hires We brought on 11 business team members and 41 Teachers this quarter with a 7.7% Turnover rate. 
7.7% turnover within the quarter is actually really solid for a growth company in education. Industry average hovers around 13-16% annually, so we're tracking well below that even with all our rapid growth. What this tells us is that when people join OpenEd, they tend to stick around. That's a direct reflection of our culture, our mission, and possibly our benefits package. 
But the insight is that with 52 total hires, losing a small percentage for various reasons means our hiring quality is strong! That being said, we still need to stay vigilant on onboarding and early-tenure engagement as that first 90 days is critical.
So, we continue to obsess over onboarding and enablement quality. And continue to recognize that sustained growth without recovery periods will eventually catch up to us, which is exactly why peer mentorship, staff development and building a resilient learning culture are priorities next quarter.
