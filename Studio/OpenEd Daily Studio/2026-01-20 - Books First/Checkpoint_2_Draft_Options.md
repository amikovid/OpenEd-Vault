### Core Argument Options

**Option A: The Sequencing Frame** GOOD
> Her approach isn't about limiting. It's about sequencing. Books came first. That's it.
>
> "From the time my first child was born, I wanted books and reading first - and have that strong foundation before they had to compete with screens."
>
> By the time screens entered the picture, the habit was already there.

**Option B: The Concrete Behavior**
> The actual rule in her house: "We turn the TV on when we're actually gonna watch something. Almost always as a family - not a lot of solo watching."
>
> That's it. No elaborate system. No app blockers. Just: books first, screens together.

**Option C: The Permission Frame**
> She's not judging you for handing your kid an iPad. She did too. The difference is what came first.
>
> You can't outcompete dopamine. But you can get there before the competition shows up.
