## Post 5: The Three Loads

**Source:** Austin Scholar - Cognitive Load Theory
**Concept extracted:** Three types of mental effort - intrinsic (concept difficulty), extraneous (unnecessary friction), germane (actual learning). Only germane helps.
**Emotional hook:** Clarifying, actionable
**Template match:** List/framework (Instagram carousel concept)

### Draft (Instagram caption for carousel)

Three types of mental effort.

Only one helps your child learn.

1. INTRINSIC - The actual difficulty of the concept
2. EXTRANEOUS - Confusing instructions, distractions, friction
3. GERMANE - Building genuine understanding

Most homework frustration is extraneous load - not the material itself.

Parents can reduce extraneous load. That frees up space for germane.
