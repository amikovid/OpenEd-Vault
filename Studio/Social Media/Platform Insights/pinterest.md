# Pinterest - Platform Insights

**Status:** Draft - needs more definition

---

## Success Heuristic

**SEO gold mine** - Pinterest is a search engine disguised as social media. Evergreen content with keyword-rich descriptions gets discovered for months/years.

---

## What Works

### Content Types
- Infographics linking to articles
- Quote cards with blog links
- Step-by-step guide pins
- Curriculum/resource roundups
- Seasonal content (back to school, etc.)

### Pin Strategy
- Every blog post = pinnable graphic
- Keyword-rich descriptions
- Proper board categorization
- Link to pillar content

---

## Anti-Patterns

- Pins without links (waste of opportunity)
- Generic descriptions without keywords
- Ignoring seasonal timing
- One-and-done pinning (need volume)

---

## Platform-Specific Voice

- Aspirational, helpful
- Solution-oriented
- Mom-friendly aesthetic
- Clean, readable graphics

---

## Specifications

- Vertical pins perform best (2:3 ratio)
- Text readable on mobile
- Strong headline on image
- Clear call to action

---

## API Notes (Get Late)
- pins:write permission
- Images with links supported
- Scheduling supported

## OpenEd-Specific Notes

- Massive opportunity for curriculum content
- Homeschool planning searches spike seasonally
- Every SEO article should have a pin
- Long-tail traffic over time
