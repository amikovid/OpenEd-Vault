# TikTok - Platform Insights

**Status:** Draft - consolidating from multiple sources

---

## Success Heuristic

**Educational entertainment** - TikTok rewards content that teaches while entertaining. The "edutainment" format works: hook them with curiosity, deliver value fast.

---

## What Works

### Content Types
- No-face formats (text, screenshots, whiteboard)
- POV videos
- Talking head with text overlay
- Trending audio + educational content
- "Hidden story" reveals

### Proven Formats (from Notion)
- "What schools don't tell you about..."
- "Hidden Genius" underdog stories
- "5 tools for X" listicles
- Education around the world comparisons
- Myth busting

### No-Face Options
- Text over stock video
- Screenshot + voiceover
- Whiteboard explainers
- AI avatar (Einstein on his upbringing, etc.)

---

## Anti-Patterns

- Text-only (video required)
- Over-produced/polished content
- Long intros before hook
- Corporate voice
- Asking for follows directly

---

## Platform-Specific Voice

- Casual, energetic
- Fast-paced delivery
- Pattern interrupts
- Slightly chaotic energy works
- Authenticity > polish

---

## Specifications

- **Video required** - Cannot post text or images only
- 9:16 vertical format
- 15-60 seconds optimal (under 3 min max)
- Hook in first 1 second
- Trending audio boosts discovery

---

## Algorithm Notes

The Triple Word Score applies:
1. Topic words spoken in audio (first 10 sec)
2. On-screen text reinforcing topic
3. Caption with keywords
4. Hashtags: 3-5 relevant tags
