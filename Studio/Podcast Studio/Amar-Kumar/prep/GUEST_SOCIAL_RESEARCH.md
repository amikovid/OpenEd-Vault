## Key Research & Data Points

KaiPod partnered with **EdChoice** on a major parent survey (March 2025):
- **76%** of parents "very satisfied" with microschooling
- **77%** of students attend part-time (blending with homeschool)
- **75%** feel learning is "extremely" or "very" personalized
- **48%** were homeschooled before enrolling in microschool
- **10%** of school parents report child attends microschool (~2M+ students nationally)

This data is highly shareable and represents Amar's thought leadership.

**Sources:**
- [EdChoice Q&A with Amar Kumar](https://www.edchoice.org/2025-diving-into-the-survey-of-microschooling-parents-a-qa-with-kaipod-learning-founder-and-ceo-amar-kumar/)
