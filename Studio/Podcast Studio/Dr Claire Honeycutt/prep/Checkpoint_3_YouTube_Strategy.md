## 3. YouTube Description

### Opening Hook (first 100 characters)

Dr. Claire Honeycutt left a tenured neuroscience career to homeschool. Her house IS the curriculum.

### Full Description

Dr. Claire Honeycutt is a neuroscientist, researcher, and homeschool parent who recommends two philosophically opposed books - Well-Trained Mind and Free to Learn - and says you need both. In this second conversation with Ela, Claire walks through exactly how she built a home where Shakespeare is a breakfast puzzle, the TV isn't the center of the room, and a pool table replaced the dining table. She explains why sensitive developmental windows matter, how she introduces rigor without killing joy, and why she thinks kids need to learn to think before they learn to prompt.
