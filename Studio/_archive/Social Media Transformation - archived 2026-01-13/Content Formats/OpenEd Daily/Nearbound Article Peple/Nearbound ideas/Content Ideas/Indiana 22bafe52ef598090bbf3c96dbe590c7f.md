### Top Candidates:

1. **Indiana Association of Home Educators (IAHE) Leadership**
    - Founded in 1983, the premier non-profit homeschool organization in Indiana
    - The IAHE is the premiere non-profit organization for Indiana homeschool information [IAHE - The Indiana Association of Home Educators Official Website](https://iahe.net/)
    - They maintain visibility with government leaders and influence legislative processes
    - Would bring institutional credibility and deep knowledge of Indiana regulations
2. **Indiana Foundation for Home Schooling Board Members**
    - Governed by a Board of Directors composed of home educators with more than seventy years of practical experience home schooling and community leaders [Indiana Foundation for Home Schooling](https://www.indianahomeschooling.org/)
    - They've served the Indiana homeschooling community for nearly 30 years
    - Created the Annual Indiana Statewide Home School Graduation
3. **Local Support Group Leaders**
    - Heritage Family Educators (Christian homeschool co-op in Corydon)
    - Greater Lafayette Home Educators Association (GLHEA)
    - Fort Wayne Area Home Schools (FWAHS) - offers quarterly newsletter and orientation classes
