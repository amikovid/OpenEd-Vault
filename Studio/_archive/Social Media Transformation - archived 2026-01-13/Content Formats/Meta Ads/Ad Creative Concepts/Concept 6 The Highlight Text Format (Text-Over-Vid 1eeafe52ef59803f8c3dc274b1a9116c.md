# Concept 6: "The Highlight Text Format" (Text-Over-Video)

Ad Type: Text over video
Status: Draft
Ad Set: OpenEd Daily
Summary: This ad concept is for "The Highlight Text Format" which features a simple video with text overlays that emphasize key points from the OpenEd Daily newsletter aimed at parents. It highlights the benefits of personalized education, encourages subscriptions, and promotes quick, actionable insights delivered daily.

**Media:** Simple video with text overlay highlighting key points, showing brief glimpses of the newsletter content

**Text Overlay Sequence:**

```
Parents from Stanford, Harvard
and innovative schools across the country
get their education insights from us.

Our daily newsletter brings you the
most important and actionable
ideas in personalized education. For free.

Join 25,000+ Educational Architects.

```

*(With key phrases in bright highlight boxes)*

**Primary Text:**
"When traditional education isn't working for your unique child, OpenEd Daily shows you the alternatives that actually work. Five minutes each morning gives you research-backed insights, practical tips, and access to a community of families who've found educational freedom."

**Headline:**
"Transform Education in 5 Minutes a Day"

**Description:**
"Free daily insights for parents"

**CTA Button:**
"Subscribe Free"

**Page:**
OpenEd Daily newsletter signup page

These concepts adapt the successful elements from the TLDR newsletter ads while tailoring the content specifically for education-minded parents seeking alternatives to traditional schooling approaches. Each maintains a focus on the quick, valuable insights delivered daily and the transformation possible through personalized education.
