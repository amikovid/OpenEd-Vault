# "This Is Your Sign" Format

Ad Type: Text over video
Status: In Review
Ad Set: OpenEd Program
Summary: This ad promotes the OpenEd program, highlighting its benefits for families considering homeschooling. It features a video of a parent transitioning from feeling overwhelmed to relieved upon discovering the program, which offers tuition-free access to educational resources and support. The ad includes a compelling text overlay, primary text emphasizing the ease of application, and a clear call to action to "Apply Now."
