# "The Educational Possibility Wall" (Kansas)

Ad Type: Text over video
Status: Draft
Ad Set: OpenEd Program
Summary: This meta ad promotes the "OpenEd Program" in Kansas, highlighting a partnership with public schools to provide educational resources like books and technology to families without financial constraints. It encourages parents to design personalized education for their children and emphasizes a quick 5-minute application process, with a call to action to start the application.

**Visual:** Kansas family looking at wall/display of educational resources (books, technology, art supplies, science kits)

**Text Overlay:**

```
Kansas families:

Your child's education shouldn't be
limited by your budget.

OpenEd's innovative program makes
these resources accessible through our
public school partnerships.

```

**Primary Text:** "Design the education that works for YOUR child without financial constraints. The Kansas OpenEd program partners with forward-thinking public schools to make personalized education possible for every family. Application takes just 5 minutes."

**CTA:** "Start Application"
