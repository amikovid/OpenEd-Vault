# "The Freedom to Choose" (Oregon)

Ad Type: Text over video
Status: Draft
Ad Set: OpenEd Program
Summary: This meta ad promotes Oregon's OpenEd program for homeschoolers, highlighting the freedom to choose diverse educational resources without budget limitations. It features visuals of Oregon's natural beauty and families selecting educational options, with a call to action for checking eligibility.

**Visual:** Simple footage of Oregon natural beauty transitioning to family selecting from different educational resources

**Text Overlay:**

```
Oregon homeschoolers, admit it:

You're tired of compromising on
educational resources due to budget constraints.

OpenEd's Oregon program removes those
barriers so you can select what works best
for YOUR unique child.

```

**Primary Text:** "Why choose just one approach when your child can benefit from the best of each? Oregon's OpenEd program works with innovative public schools to make premium educational resources accessible to approved families."

**CTA:** "Check Eligibility"
