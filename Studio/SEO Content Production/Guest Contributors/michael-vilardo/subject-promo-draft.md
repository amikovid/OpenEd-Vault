## Newsletter Segment Draft

### High School Just Got a Lot More Flexible

One question we get constantly: *"What do I do when my kids hit high school?"*

Traditional homeschool curriculum gets harder to pull off. Transcripts matter. Accreditation matters. And honestly? Teaching AP Chemistry yourself isn't for everyone.

**Subject.com** is solving this. They offer 100+ accredited high school courses - everything from Algebra to AP Psychology - that your student can take on their own schedule.

A few things that stood out to us:
- **Actually accredited** (Cognia) - transcripts colleges recognize
- **Self-paced** - start anytime, finish when ready
- **Video-first** - built for how students actually learn
- **ESA-friendly** - accepted by most state programs

The founder, Michael Vilardo, has a story that resonates: he attended 7 different schools in 5 years growing up, became his town's first Ivy League graduate, and built Subject to give other kids the flexibility he wished he'd had.

Worth checking out if high school is on your horizon: [Subject.com](https://subject.com)
