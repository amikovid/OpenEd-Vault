## Competitive Landscape

### Top Ranking Articles

**1. iHomeschool Network - "Comparing Homeschooling Methods"**
- Format: Multi-method comparison (Charlotte Mason, Montessori, Waldorf)
- Strength: Comprehensive overview
- Gap: Surface-level, no unique perspective

**2. Various Mommy Blogs**
- Format: Personal experience comparisons
- Strength: Authentic voice
- Gap: Not data-backed, often outdated

**3. School Websites**
- Format: Marketing-focused
- Strength: Detailed on one method
- Gap: Biased toward their method

### What's Missing in Current Rankings

1. **Honest treatment of Waldorf's spiritual roots** - Most articles avoid or minimize anthroposophy
2. **Practitioner perspective** - No interviews with teachers who've done both
3. **Neurodiversity angle** - Which is better for kids with ADHD, autism, sensory needs?
4. **Technology question** - How does each handle screens in 2026?
5. **Hybrid approaches** - Can you combine them? How?
6. **Reading timing evidence** - Data on delayed reading outcomes
7. **Modern adaptations** - How have both evolved from founders' visions?

---

## Content Gap Analysis

### What Competitors Do

1. List features of each method side-by-side
2. Provide "best for" recommendations
3. Include history/biography of founders
4. Offer curriculum recommendations

### What They Miss

1. **The philosophical depth** - WHY these methods differ, not just HOW
2. **Real parent stories** - Not theoretical, lived experience
3. **The "golden nugget" framework** - Taking what works from each
4. **Evidence-based claims** - Most assertions are unsupported
5. **Internal links to deeper content** - We can link to full Montessori article

## Featured Snippet Opportunities

**Definition snippet (target: "what is waldorf education"):**
> Waldorf education is a holistic approach developed by Rudolf Steiner that emphasizes artistic expression, rhythm, and developmental stages. Children learn through storytelling, handwork, and nature connection, with academics introduced gradually based on age-appropriate readiness.

**Comparison snippet (target: "waldorf vs montessori"):**

| Aspect | Waldorf | Montessori |
|--------|---------|------------|
| Founded | 1919 by Rudolf Steiner | 1907 by Maria Montessori |
| Philosophy | Spiritual/artistic | Scientific/observational |
| Materials | Natural, handmade | Purpose-designed, specific |
| Technology | Delayed/limited | Tool-based, flexible |
| Reading | Formal instruction at 7-8 | Emerges naturally by 6-7 |
| Teacher Role | Guide through stages | Observer in prepared environment |
