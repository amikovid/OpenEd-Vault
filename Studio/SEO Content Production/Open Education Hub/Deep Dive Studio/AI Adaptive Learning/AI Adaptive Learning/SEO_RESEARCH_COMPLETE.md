## 10. Writing Guidelines for This Article

### DO:
- Lead with Alpha School proof point
- Quote experts by name with credentials
- Explain the science (but accessibly)
- Give honest limitations
- Provide implementation framework
- Address cost directly

### DON'T:
- Start with abstract definitions
- Use generic "experts say"
- Claim everything is perfect
- Just list tools without context
- Ignore the money question
- Use AI-ism phrases

### Voice:
- Permission-giving, not prescriptive
- "You can use this when..." not "You must..."
- Specific over generic (names, numbers, examples)
- Conversational but substantive
- Honest about tradeoffs
