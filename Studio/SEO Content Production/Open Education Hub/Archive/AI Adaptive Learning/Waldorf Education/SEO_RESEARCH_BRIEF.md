## Recommended Content Strategy

### Option A: Comprehensive Waldorf Guide (Pillar)

**Title:** "Waldorf Homeschooling: A Complete Guide for Parents"
**Target:** 2,500-3,500 words
**Primary KW:** "what is waldorf education" + "waldorf homeschool"

**Outline:**
1. What Is Waldorf Education? (definition, philosophy)
2. The Rudolf Steiner Foundation (history, three 7-year cycles)
3. Core Waldorf Principles (block learning, rhythm, arts integration)
4. Waldorf vs. Traditional School (key differences)
5. How to Start Waldorf Homeschooling (practical steps)
6. Best Waldorf Curriculum Options (Oak Meadow, Christopherus, Live Education)
7. Waldorf by Age (preschool, elementary, high school)
8. Common Challenges & Solutions (burnout, tech, multi-grade)
9. FAQ (addresses PAA questions)
