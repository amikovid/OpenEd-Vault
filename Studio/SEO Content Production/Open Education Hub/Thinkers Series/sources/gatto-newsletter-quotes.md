From external research (verify before using):

**On his books:**
- *Dumbing Us Down* (1992) - Collection of essays including "Seven Lesson Schoolteacher"
- *The Underground History of American Education* (2001) - Full critique
- *Weapons of Mass Instruction* (2008) - Later work

**On his teaching style:**
- Sent students on solo research missions
- Had 13-year-olds interview legislators
- Connected "at-risk" kids with real-world experiences
- Won awards specifically for success with "difficult" students

**Biographical:**
- Born September 15, 1935, Monongahela, Pennsylvania
- Died October 25, 2018, Manhattan
- Taught 30 years in NYC public schools
- 3x NYC Teacher of the Year, 1x NYS Teacher of the Year
- Columbia University graduate
- Pre-teaching: taxi driver, ad copywriter, speechwriter
